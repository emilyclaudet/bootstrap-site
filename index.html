About


I'm Emily, and I love building products and games.

I am currently based in Berlin and work as a Product Manager at HelloFresh where I am making supply chain software. Before that I was a Product Manager at Kano in London, building a computer kit to introduce kids to computing.

Outside of work, I love making (and playing!) video games. I am working with my sister and brother on several projects, our most recent one being "Through the Dark Realm". This was built during the Nordic Game Jam in April 2017.

Product at Kano


I worked at Kano from December 2013 - August 2016. I saw it grow from a $1.5 million Kickstarter campaign to a $15 million series A company. The goal was to create a computer kit that inspired kids to create with technology. I loved the challenge of doing both hardware and software as it involved working cross functionally across engineering, supply chain and design.

  • Website: kano.me

Tech Speaker


I enjoy speaking at conferences, especially to young people about technology. For International Womens Day 2016, I spoke at Campus London for Acorn Aspirations about my experience getting into Tech with Kano. I have also spoken for Geek Girl Carrots and at UCL on the working at startups after university.
